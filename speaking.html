Speaking

Topics & Workshops

Every talk is tailored to the audience and the occasion. These are the areas I return to most, the ideas I care about and have spent years thinking through.

01

Public Speaking & Confidence

How to move past fear, structure your thinking, and deliver ideas that land. Drawn from years of teaching debate and coaching students through their first public talks. Practical, grounded, and immediately applicable.

02

Leadership Through Communication

The best leaders are clear communicators. This talk explores how to lead by articulating vision, listening carefully, and creating space for others to contribute. These are skills that matter in boardrooms, classrooms, and communities alike.

03

Finding Your Voice in Competitive Environments

From debate tournaments to law school to professional settings. How to develop the confidence to speak up when the stakes are high and the room is not always built for you.

04

Communication Skills for Students

A workshop-style talk designed for high school and university audiences. Covers structuring arguments, reading a room, managing nerves, and the long-term value of learning to communicate well early.

05

Law, Entrepreneurship & Agency

What law school teaches you about building things, and what building things teaches you about the law. A talk about agency, risk, and the intersection of legal thinking and entrepreneurial ambition.
