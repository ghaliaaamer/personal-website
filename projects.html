Projects

Building, not just advising.

Before studying law, Ghalia founded TalkMaze, an internationally recognized organization focused on public speaking and debate education. Building TalkMaze from the ground up taught her what it means to operate under uncertainty, make decisions with limited information, and communicate a vision to people who need to believe in it.

That experience informs everything she does, from how she teaches communication to how she thinks about the founders she works with. Understanding what it feels like to build something gives her talks a grounded, practical perspective that resonates with entrepreneurial audiences.
